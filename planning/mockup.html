Design notes

  • Month switcher, or a continuous scroll?
  • How far back should people be able to change their responses? Three days? I want to build in time for people to forget a day without defaulting to skipped, but I also don't want people to be able to go back and edit what they said a month ago.
  • Not sure how to handle the UI for adding or viewing notes. I like the simplicity of click the calendar day to toggle through practice options, but I'm not sure what to do differently for notes. Is it too small just to link the note icon? Probably.
  • Do I want to give them a "partial" option? I did last year, but I think it gives them too much rope. Even if I do, I should count the partial as a no for the purposes of my own backend.
